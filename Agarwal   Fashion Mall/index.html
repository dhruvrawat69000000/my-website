Our Story

A Legacy Built on Fashion & Trust

Located in the heart of Khelari, Jharkhand, Agarwal Fashion Mall is a modern fashion destination built on the values of trust, quality, and affordability.

What started as a small apparel store has now transformed into a spacious, full-fledged fashion mall, offering the latest trends and timeless styles for men, women, and children — all under one roof.

Our Journey

75 Years of Excellence

A legacy built with passion, trust, and dedication to fashion.

1950s
Humble Beginnings
A small apparel store opens in Khelari, Jharkhand — serving the local community with quality fabric and honest service.
1980s
Growing Trust
Word spreads across the region. Agarwal Fashion becomes the go-to destination for festive shopping for thousands of families.
2000s
Major Expansion
Store expands significantly, adding dedicated sections for women, men, and children with a wider range of brands and styles.
2020s
Fashion Mall Era
Complete transformation into a full-fledged Fashion Mall — modern infrastructure, curated collections, and an elevated shopping experience.
Today
75 Years & Counting
Jharkhand's beloved fashion landmark. Premium styles, affordable prices, and the trust of generations — all under one roof.
